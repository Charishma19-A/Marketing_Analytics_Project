# Marketing Analytics Project

## Overview
This project simulates a real-world enterprise marketing analytics system focused on customer journey analytics, campaign performance optimization, CRM lifecycle tracking, segmentation, and email marketing analysis.

It was designed to replicate practical business scenarios similar to large-scale retail and digital marketing organizations.

---

## Business Objective
To integrate and analyze customer and campaign data across multiple channels in order to improve:

- Campaign performance
- Funnel visibility
- Customer lifecycle understanding
- Audience segmentation
- Email nurture effectiveness
- Reporting automation
- Marketing decision-making
