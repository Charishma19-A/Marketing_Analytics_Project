# Marketing Analytics Project

## Project Name:
Customer Journey Analytics and Campaign Performance Optimization

## Business Problem:
Customer and campaign data was spread across multiple platforms such as Google Ads, GA4, Salesforce CRM, HubSpot, and Mailchimp. This created limited visibility into customer journeys, campaign effectiveness, funnel performance, and retention opportunities.

## Objective:
To integrate and analyze multi-channel marketing and customer data in order to improve:
- Campaign performance
- Customer journey visibility
- Funnel conversion
- CRM lifecycle understanding
- Customer segmentation
- Reporting efficiency

## Tools Used:
- Python
- SQL
- Excel
- Google Ads
- GA4
- Salesforce CRM
- HubSpot
- Mailchimp
- Power BI (planned dashboard layer)

## Key Analysis Performed:
### Paid Campaign Analysis:
- CTR
- CPC
- CPA
- ROAS

### Funnel Analysis:
- Session start
- Page view
- Product view
- Form start
- Form submit
- Drop-off analysis

### CRM Analysis:
- Lead stages
- MQL
- SQL
- Opportunity
- Converted

### Email Analysis:
- Open rate
- Click rate
- Conversion rate
- Unsubscribe rate

### Segmentation Analysis:
- High Intent
- Loyal Customer
- Inactive Customer
- Engagement scoring

## Business Outcomes:
- Improved campaign visibility
- Better funnel optimization insights
- Enhanced segmentation opportunities
- CRM lifecycle understanding
- Stronger reporting automation
- Data-driven marketing decision support

## Interview Summary:
I built a marketing analytics project that integrated and analyzed customer journey, campaign, CRM, segmentation, and email performance data across multiple systems. Using SQL and Python, I automated KPI reporting, funnel analysis, segmentation, and lifecycle tracking to support better marketing optimization and decision-making.
